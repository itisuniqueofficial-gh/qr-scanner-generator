# Play Store Listing Draft

## App Title

QR Scanner & Generator

## Short Description

Scan, read, and generate QR codes instantly.

## Full Description

QR Scanner & Generator is a fast, simple, and lightweight app for scanning, reading, and creating QR codes directly from your device.

Use your camera to scan QR codes, upload QR images to decode them, generate QR codes for everyday sharing, create UPI payment QR codes, and open UPI payment links in supported apps.

Built for speed, clarity, and ease of use, the app keeps the experience clean and practical without extra clutter.

### Features

- Scan QR codes using your camera
- Upload and read QR code images
- Generate QR codes for text, links, phone, email, SMS, and Wi-Fi
- Create UPI payment QR codes
- Open UPI payment links in supported apps
- Fast, lightweight, and responsive
- Privacy-friendly browser-powered experience

## Why Use It

- Clean and easy interface
- Quick access to common QR tools
- Works well on modern Android devices
- Useful for links, contact sharing, Wi-Fi access, payments, and more
- No unnecessary visual clutter
