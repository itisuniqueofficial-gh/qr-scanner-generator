# Play Store Listing Draft

## App Title

QR Scanner & Generator

## Short Description

Scan, read, and generate QR codes instantly.

## Full Description

A fast and simple QR Scanner, QR Reader, QR Code Generator, and UPI QR utility app built for everyday use.

### Features

- Scan QR codes using your camera
- Upload and read QR code images
- Generate QR codes for text, links, phone, email, SMS, and Wi-Fi
- Create UPI payment QR codes
- Open UPI payment links in supported apps
- Fast, lightweight, and responsive
- Privacy-friendly browser-powered experience

## Privacy Policy

https://qr.itisuniqueofficial.com/privacy-policy.html

## Notes For Play Console

- Camera is used only for QR scanning
- Image access is used only for QR image upload and reading
- UPI payments are handled by external payment apps, not by this app directly
