Privacy Policy

Privacy information in plain language

QR Scanner & Generator is designed to keep the experience simple and respectful.

Camera access

Camera access is only requested on the scanner page when you choose to start scanning. It is used only to read QR codes from the live camera view.

Local processing

QR content is processed locally in your browser where possible. Uploaded images and scanned frames are used to detect QR codes for your current session.

Data storage

This website does not aim to store unnecessary personal data.
