About

Useful QR tools without extra clutter

QR Scanner & Generator is a simple static website that helps people scan QR codes, read QR codes from images, and generate new QR codes for everyday sharing.

What this tool does

You can use the scanner for live camera scanning, the reader for uploaded screenshots and photos, and the generator for text, links, phone numbers, email, SMS, and Wi-Fi setup details.

Why it is useful

QR codes save time. They make it easier to open websites, share contact details, connect to Wi-Fi, and pass along information in a format that works across devices.

Privacy-friendly by design

This website is built to keep the experience simple and respectful. Camera access is only needed for live scanning, and local browser processing is used where possible for QR tasks.
